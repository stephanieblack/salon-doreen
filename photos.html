Doreen's Mission

Doreen believes firmly that knowing your hair looks fabulous gives you the confidence to take on new challenges either at work or in a social setting. You can step out knowing your look is flattering to you and you have your own style that is beyond fabulous. Doreen makes a personal connection with all of her clients and makes it her goal to ensure that each client leaves looking confidently their best.

Your Appointment

Doreen understands that your appearance is important to you and makes it her goal to address your needs and concerns to ensure that you leave feeling better about yourself and your hair. Doreen listens to each and every client to discover their lifestyle, time constraints, and concerns they may have. After assessing your needs, she'll provide a cut, color, or style that will make your hair the best it can be.
